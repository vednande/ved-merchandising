How Sortler works

Sortler is a cloud based tool for merchandiser and eCommerce professionals.

Drag and Drop catalog management

Use the Drag and Drop function to sort your products in the most intutive, quickest way.

Set scores for products by adding numbers, SKUs with higher scores will be displayed on top of the category pages.

Achieve a certain vareity on
your catalog pages

Manage display of product groups per category pages:

  • Specify the maximun number of identical product groups per pages: subcategories, brands attributes(like color,style or type).
  • Hide and add the desired products or product groups on the category page.

Control poor-selling products

Sortler provides flexible features for the management of your low-selling items.

Use a convenient filter to see all low-selling items in your assortment. Knowing the situation, you can take appropriate measures in time to prevent possible financial damage.
